# Physical AI Fundamentals

Welcome to **Physical AI Fundamentals** — a comprehensive textbook designed to guide you through the exciting convergence of robotics, artificial intelligence, and simulation technologies.

## About This Textbook

This textbook provides a structured journey through the technologies that power modern intelligent robotic systems. From the foundational concepts of embodied intelligence to cutting-edge Vision-Language-Action models, you'll gain both theoretical understanding and practical knowledge.

## Who Is This For?

This textbook is designed for:

- **Robotics engineers** looking to integrate AI into their systems
- **AI/ML practitioners** wanting to apply their skills to physical systems
- **Students** in robotics, computer science, or related fields
- **Researchers** exploring embodied intelligence and physical AI
- **Industry professionals** building next-generation autonomous systems

## Prerequisites

To get the most from this textbook, you should have:

- Basic programming experience (Python preferred)
- Familiarity with linear algebra and calculus fundamentals
- Understanding of basic machine learning concepts
- Curiosity about robotics and intelligent systems

## Textbook Structure

### Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI
Understand what Physical AI is, how it differs from traditional AI, and explore the evolution from industrial robotics to intelligent machines.

### Chapter 2: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)
Master ROS 2 — the communication backbone that connects perception, planning, and control in modern robotic systems.

### Chapter 3: Digital Twin and Simulation
Learn physics-based simulation with NVIDIA Isaac Sim, Gazebo, and MuJoCo. Generate synthetic data and understand sim-to-real transfer.

### Chapter 4: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)
Explore NVIDIA Isaac for GPU-accelerated perception, deep learning inference, and edge deployment on Jetson platforms.

### Chapter 5: Vision-Language-Action Systems
Discover how foundation models combining vision, language, and action are revolutionizing robot capabilities and enabling natural human-robot interaction.

## Learning Approach

Each chapter includes:

- **Learning Objectives** — Clear goals for what you'll achieve
- **Conceptual Explanations** — Theory and background
- **Code Examples** — Practical implementations
- **Diagrams and Tables** — Visual aids for complex concepts
- **Summary** — Key takeaways to reinforce learning
- **Additional Resources** — Papers, tutorials, and tools for deeper exploration
